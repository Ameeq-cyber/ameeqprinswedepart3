About UniBuddy

UniBuddy is focused solely on college and university students and mainly first years to navigate their academics and maintain a good well-being during their first year. It helps students manage their time efficiently and improve overall productivity levels. UniBuddy was founded in 2026 by a group of college students who presented the idea for a business pitch in their business management class.

Mission

UniBuddy's mission is to be helpful in assisting students to excel academically through using creative tools and one of a kind features.

Vision

UniBuddy's vision is to become one of the best student productivity platforms and soon thereafter the leading student productivity platform for all college and university students and not just only first years.

Target Users

University and college students, first-years needing guidance, study groups and campus communities, and students wanting better organisation.

Website goals

The goals of UniBuddy are to provide students with the necessary academic support and promote student collaboration. Also to create an offline mode to access UniBuddy without internet and cater to a diverse student population.
